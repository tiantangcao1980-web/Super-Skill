# Voice Tone Matrix

Use four dials before writing or rewriting product copy:

| Dial | Low | High |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Warmth | precise, restrained | companionable, encouraging |
| Authority | exploratory | decisive |
| Energy | calm | punchy |
| Specificity | broad | concrete |

Pick one dominant voice and one secondary flex. Avoid mixing all four highs at once; it usually reads like generic marketing.

## Quick Prompts

- Who is speaking?
- What does the user already believe?
- What should the user do next?
- What emotion should be reduced: confusion, risk, effort, or boredom?
