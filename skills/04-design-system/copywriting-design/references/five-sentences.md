# Five Sentences

Every product surface usually needs five high-leverage sentences:

1. Main promise: the concrete outcome.
2. Primary CTA: the next action.
3. Empty state: why nothing is here yet.
4. Error state: what happened and how to recover.
5. Success state: what changed and what comes next.

## Anti-Patterns

- "Effortlessly", "seamlessly", and "revolutionary" without proof.
- Fake numbers and fake social proof.
- Clever headlines that hide the actual product value.
- Error messages that blame the user.
